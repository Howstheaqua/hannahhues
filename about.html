About Page

Me

My name is Hannah Grubb. I am a student at the University of North Carolina at Charlotte. I have been drawing since I was a child and have always had a passion for art. I have been working on my art skills for the past 10 years and have been able to improve my skills. I have been able to create a variety of different art styles.

Mission Statement

A goal that have as an Illustrator is to be well-rounded and multifaceted. The way that know I'm on the right track is that when start a piece, the world around me disappears. I don't answer my phone, and the hours slip past me. I really love creating. Usually I am a perfectionist, so I keep on doing my work until am satisfied. I cannot just give up or say "Well, guess that's good enough"-- It has to be up to my standard. I also am able to get things done relatively quickly, as I get in a groove when I start projects.
